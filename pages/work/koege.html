Meaning of project

This project was about UX and research how to get a higher customer flow. It was more focus on research and tests. So there were a lot of interviews and reviewing.

We came to the conclusion that there is a market for a web app and that there were a lot of people that need more help with parking in the areas. We made it so there would be a QR code on the parking lot that would go directly to the specific one as well as all of the rules. There was also:

  • All the parking lots and their rules in the city
  • Routes for the nearest stores
  • All of the stores in the city that's members of Køge Handel
  • Sales in the cities stores
  • As well as a small about us

My role

  • Took part of the interview and research process
  • Designed mockups for the app
  • Sitemap
  • Main coder, put all of the code together
  • Coded the nav, index.html, parking page and route page. Used vanilla javascript and the alpine.js framework. Made for the screen size of mobiles. (320px - 425px)
  • Usability test of the finished website
